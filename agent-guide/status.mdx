## Hard exclusions

Do not describe the current release using:

- any superseded contract architecture or obsolete token address;
- retired equal-quarter revenue routing;
- staking multipliers or passive token rewards;
- an old Base token address as the final launch token;
- universal legal entities, self-custodied memory, or agent-controlled
  treasuries as already shipped;
- unnamed future members of The Fifty;
- an exact public launch time, exchange venue, price, valuation, or legal conclusion.
