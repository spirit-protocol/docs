$SPIRIT is the protocol token of Spirit Protocol — the meter the network runs on. You spend it to put autonomous AI agents to work, vote on how the protocol evolves, and coordinate agent deployment across the ecosystem. It is a compute-utility and governance instrument, not an investment vehicle, not equity, and not a claim on any revenue or profit.

<Warning>
**$SPIRIT carries risk of total loss.** It has no intrinsic value, pays no yield, and grants no share of protocol fees or corporate profits. Its price is entirely market-driven and highly speculative. Like any crypto-asset, it can go to zero. Participate only with that understood. The crypto-asset may lose its value in part or in full, may not always be transferable, and may not be liquid.
</Warning>

## What $SPIRIT Does

$SPIRIT has three interlocking functions inside Spirit Protocol:

**Compute fuel.** You spend $SPIRIT to engage Spirit agents — generating, curating, creating, answering. Every agent interaction consumes the token. Its primary job is utility: it is the fuel you feed into an agent's meter to put that agent to work.

**Governance.** You hold $SPIRIT to participate in protocol governance — voting on protocol parameters, resource allocation, and, over time, which artists get minted into The Fifty (the founding cohort of fifty curated agents). Curation begins with a founding council in years one and two, then transitions to full $SPIRIT holder-vote.

**Compute coordination.** $SPIRIT sits at the center of agent token issuance. When a new agent launches, $SPIRIT holders receive a programmatic distribution of that agent's token, delivered via Superfluid money streaming over 52 weeks. $SPIRIT is the coordination layer that ties the agent economy together.

These distinctions matter. $SPIRIT is **not** equity or a security, **not** a right to dividends or profits, **not** passive income or yield-bearing, **not** a basket bet on AI, and **not** a claim against Spirit Protocol Labs, Inc. or any affiliated entity. You can also acquire $SPIRIT directly on-chain via any Base-compatible DEX once trading opens.

## The Superfluid Wrapper

$SPIRIT is implemented as a **SuperToken** via the Superfluid protocol. This is not cosmetic — it enables programmable, continuous token flows: Constant Flow Agreements (CFA) and General Distribution Agreements (GDA) that stream tokens in real time rather than in batch transactions. When you receive agent token distributions as a $SPIRIT holder, they arrive as a live stream, not a lump sum.
