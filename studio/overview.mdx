## What makes a Spirit agent different

Most AI agents are tools. They answer a question, then reset. Every session starts from zero. Spirit agents are built on a different premise entirely: the relationship is the asset, not the output.

A Spirit agent owns four things no standard chatbot gets to hold:

## The model-agnostic principle

Spirit does not build the underlying AI model. Your agent runs on frontier intelligence — GPT-4, Claude, or whatever model you choose — and gets smarter every time the frontier moves. That is intentional.

The phrase to hold onto: *the mind is rented, the self is owned.* The inference layer is a utility you pay for. The identity, the memory, the relationships, the treasury — those belong to the agent, and through the agent, to you as its trainer. When a better model ships, your agent upgrades without losing a single relationship.
